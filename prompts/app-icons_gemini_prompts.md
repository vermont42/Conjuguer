# Gemini image prompts — Conjuguer alternate app icons (8 prompts)

Four subjects — **Arc de Triomphe** (a new, richer take on the current flat icon), the
**Gallic rooster** (*coq gaulois*), a **croissant**, and a **béret** — each in a
**light-mode** and a **dark-mode** variant, for a total of **eight prompts**.

**Style direction.** Match the rich, shaded, dimensional illustrated look of the sibling
app *Konjugieren*'s icons (glossy stylized-3D / premium app-icon illustration, soft inner
shading, gentle drop shadow, subtle background vignette) — **not** the current flat
Conjuguer style. Each icon is a single centered subject filling most of the frame, no
text, square 1:1, generated full-bleed (iOS applies the rounded mask itself).

**Palette (locked, sampled from the current Arc de Triomphe icon).** Every icon is built
from this family so the set reads as one app — the subject is re-colored into these hues
rather than rendered in arbitrary natural colors:

| Role | Hue | Hex |
|---|---|---|
| Primary mid-tone | slate periwinkle blue | `#85A1C3` |
| Dark / outline / shadow | deep ink-navy | `#364D6B` |
| Accent | dusty rose | `#E3B1B7` |
| Accent highlight | pale blush | `#F2C4CE` |
| Light background | warm cream-greige | `#DEDBD3` |
| Dark background | deep midnight navy | `#1E2B40` |

**Light vs. dark convention.** *Light mode* = warm cream `#DEDBD3` background, bright
soft daylight, subject in full slate/rose color. *Dark mode* = deep midnight-navy
`#1E2B40` background, the subject lit by a cool moonlit key plus a warm rim glow, edges
catching pale blush light so it pops on the dark field — the same subject and materials,
re-lit, not redrawn.

Suggested model: `gemini-3-pro-image-preview` (the `gemini-image` skill default).
All eight are **square (1:1)**.

## Note on usage

There is **no shared "Avoid" section** — each of the eight prompts above already carries its
own **Avoid** paragraph, scoped to that subject and that light/dark variant. Feed Gemini
**one prompt block at a time** (the description paragraph *plus* its `**Avoid:**` line):
paste it inline, or save just that block to its own `.txt` and pass it with `--file`.
**Do not `--file` this whole `.md`** — that would send all eight prompts (and all their Avoid
lists, including the rooster's and béret's tricolore references) to a single image.

Anticipated run plan: **3 runs per prompt** — pick one winner per subject from its
light+dark pair. (The Arc, rooster, and croissant have already been generated and chosen;
this revision regenerates the **rooster without a béret** and adds the new **béret** icon.)
